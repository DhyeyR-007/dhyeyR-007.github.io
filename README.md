### 👋 About me

I'm a PhD candidate at the **[QUT Centre for Robotics](https://research.qut.edu.au/qcr/)**, working with [Prof. Michael Milford](https://research.qut.edu.au/centre-for-future-enterprise/people/michael-milford/) and [Dr. Tobias Fischer](https://www.tobiasfischer.info/). My research is about making robots know *where they are* — reliably, in the real world, without GPS.

- 🔭 **Research:** Visual Place Recognition · SLAM · Perception for Physical AI
- 🎓 **Education:** PhD @ QUT · M.S. Robotics @ University of Michigan Ann Arbor
- 📍 **Location:** Brisbane, Australia (UTC +10:00)
- 📄 **Latest paper:** [Quantile Transfer for Reliable Operating Point Selection in VPR](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04401) — arXiv 2025
